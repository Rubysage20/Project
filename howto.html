How to Practice Meditation

Mindfulness
To practice mindfulness : Take your time when you are responding to external stimuli. Before you respond ask yourself is your response constructed in a way of empathy, compassion, and kindfulness.

Still Meditation
Sit or lie down in a relaxing position and focus on your breathing, inhale to a count of 4, hold for a count of 4, and exhale for a count of 4. Practice daily for atleast 20 minutes

Open-Eye Meditation
Sit or lie in front of a blank wall ( this limits distractions), Find a focus point, Keep your eyes open, Blinking is okay. Open eye meditation is similar to day dreaming.

Yoga Meditation
This form of meditation strengthens the body and mind.
